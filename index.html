SharkViz: Shark Finder

Welcome to SharkViz!

SharkViz is a brand new web map intended to help visualize which beaches may have a higher chance of a shark-human interaction. This is not a tool designed to show the most "dangerous" beaches, but rather those that are potentially more likely for a shark to swim close to, and those that may provide ecological benefits to sharks. Although there are over 500 species of sharks, this project focuses mainly on Great White sharks, which were tagged in the Atlantic Ocean near the East Coast of the United States.

A megalodon-sized thank you to Chris Fischer, John Tyminski, and the rest of the Ocearch research team for sharing their shark data. This project would not have been possible without their help and support. One more thank you to the 25 sharks that provided their journeys to me. Just keep swimming!

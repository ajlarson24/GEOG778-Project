SharkViz: Shark Finder

Welcome to SharkViz!

SharkViz is a brand new web map intended to help visualize which beaches may have a higher chance of a shark-human interaction. This is not a tool designed to show the most "dangerous" beaches, but rather those that are potentially more likely for a shark to swim close to, and those that may provide ecological benefits to sharks.

A megalodon-sized thank you to Chris Fischer, John Tyminski, and the rest of the Ocearch research team for sharing their shark data. This project would not have been possible without their help and support.
